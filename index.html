Crafted with
Intention.

We believe great clothes should last a lifetime — not a season. Each piece is designed to be worn, washed, and worn again.

Born from a belief in less, but better.

MAISON was founded in 2018 by Elise Martin, a former textile designer who grew tired of the relentless pace of fast fashion. She wanted to create a brand that valued craft over trend, permanence over novelty.

Every garment begins as a sketch, moves through multiple rounds of fabric sourcing and prototyping, and is only released when the team is genuinely proud to wear it themselves.

We work with a small network of family-run factories across Portugal, Japan, and Peru — chosen for their expertise, fair labour practices, and shared commitment to quality.
